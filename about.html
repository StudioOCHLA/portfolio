About me

"There is always something new to learn"

I’m a designer and communicator living in Skåne, Sweden. I create interfaces that are both beautiful and functional, improving people's lives. For the past two years, I have been studying diverse aspects of design, such as user research, UX, and visual design, and implementing these approaches to issues of all shapes and sizes.

I am curious, and love to learn new things.

Thru my cultural & humanities studies, I have learned to understand human behavior and digital artifacts, FrontEnd and visual communication.

Color in all its richness and complexity is something that often fascinates and inspires me in my work.

Lina, an explorative human being.

I’m also a creative generalist. I have always been interested in art, design, and computers. Growing up, creativity was always present.

Outside of work, I enjoy telling stories through a variety of creative media, including painting, drawing, photography, and dressmaking.

X

Working as a UX and interaction designer, I love to improve peoples everyday lives.

My work focuses on helping and aiding people, making things easier for them.

To succeed with this I use tools learned by my studies in human-centered, UX-, UI-, interaction & co-design.

Performance art and storytelling

As a side note, I am fascinated by stage productions and performance art and have always been. There is beauty in the x of the x and the in the x.

The art of storytelling is crucial

Storytelling has a big part in stage productions and I lay much focus on that in my design work.

Graphic design is not just a surface-centric practise, but it can also be a place for participation and action, insipirational or for a chance-driven act.
